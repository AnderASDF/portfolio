Who I am

to be fluid.

Born in a coastal city and a lifelong swimmer, fluidity shapes both my character and my digital workflow. As a concept artist and 3D generalist, I treat pipelines as adaptive environments, bridging 2D ideation—silhouettes, line art, colour finals—with full 3D implementation in Blender and Unreal Engine.

This fluid approach allows me to take any form a project requires, moving seamlessly across stylized, dark fantasy, and horror aesthetics. My technical and artistic adaptability spans building characters from thumbnail to 3D model, designing weapons with silhouette-first pipelines, and shipping live maps on Fortnite and Roblox. Currently contributing as VFX & Weapon Concept Artist on an indie title with a 15-person team.

Looking for a full-time position as a concept artist — ideally in a studio that values iterative design, creative collaboration, and strong world-building.
